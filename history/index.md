# History

Welcome to the History section of ChenBook, exploring key events, figures, and movements that have shaped our world.

## What You'll Find Here

This section covers historical topics across different eras and regions, providing context and analysis to deepen understanding of the past.

### Core Topics

- **Ancient History**: Early civilizations, classical antiquity, and foundational empires
- **Medieval Period**: Feudalism, cultural exchange, and the rise of major institutions
- **Modern History**: Revolutions, industrialization, and the formation of nation-states
- **Contemporary History**: Global conflicts, technological change, and the modern world order
- **Regional Histories**: In-depth looks at specific regions and their unique trajectories

### Learning Approach

Each topic includes:
- **Core Articles**: Well-researched summaries with historical context
- **Question Bank**: Questions to test and reinforce understanding
- **Interactive Flashcards**: Key dates, figures, and concepts for review
